About the data

Read this page to see how solar energy generation and total energy consumption compare for three major California energy utilities. You'll notice that much more energy is consumed than generated by solar. The remaining portion of energy comprises a mix of renewable, fossil fuel, and nuclear sources. This website only shows the California CSI working data, but you can. learn more about California's energy sources.

The graphs and tables on this page show CSI data specifically from {{year}}. Try visiting different years to see how they compare.
