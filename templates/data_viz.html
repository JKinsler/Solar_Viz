About the data

Solar energy capacity has been increasingly added to the California energy grid since the early 2000s.

The State of California tracks the progress of renewable energy by growth through the California Solar Initiative. In this program, the State partners with energy utilities to track solar energy production data. Gnergy generation data was tracked for several years by three companies. This data is called the 'CSI working dataset'. This page shows CSI participation from three of California's large energy utilities: Pacific Gas and Electric, San Diego Gas and Electric and, Southern California Edison.
