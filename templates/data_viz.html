Annual Solar Energy Generation


About the data

The State of California tracks the growth of solar energy generation through the California Solar Initiative (CSI). In this program, the state partners with energy utilities to gather data on solar energy production. This data is called the "CSI working dataset". This page shows CSI participation from three of California's large energy utilities: Pacific Gas and Electric, San Diego Gas and Electric, and Southern California Edison.


Annual solar energy generation, from California CSI dataset

The chart above shows the combined solar energy production from CSI's participating utilties. The downward trend after 2016 might be accounted for by the end of reporting on specific projects. Actual solar capacity in California has not declined. Visit the California Solar Energy Statistics and Data, to learn more about current solar energy production.
